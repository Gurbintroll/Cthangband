Chaos Spells

Spells are presented here ordered by book. This does not necessarily reflect how difficult each spell is to cast. Although within the first two books (Sign of Chaos and Chaos Mastery) the spells are ordered in increasing difficulty, each of the two rare books that can only be found in the dungeons (G'harne Fragments and The Book of Azathoth) contain both easy and difficult spells.

The level at which each individual spell is unlocked and the mana cost to cast each spell is not given here, since it will vary with each class that has access to this realm.

Sign of Chaos

a) Magic Missile
Creates a physical missile which strikes a nearby target.

b) Trap / Door Destruction
Destroys any adjacent doors or traps.

c) Flash of Light
Permanently lights the nearby area, possibly damaging creatures.

d) Touch of Confusion
Creates an aura around your hands that will confuse the next creature you attack.

e) Mana Burst
Fires a ball of raw magical (not elemental) energy.

f) Fire Bolt
Fires a bolt of flame that does fire damage.

g) Fist of Force
Fires a bolt of pure force, that can disintegrate what it touches.

h) Teleport Self
Teleports you to a random place on the level.

Chaos Mastery

a) Wonder
Creates a random (but often useful) chaotic effect. The more powerful you are, the more useful the effect is likely to be.

b) Chaos Bolt
Fires a bolt of raw chaos.

c) Sonic Boom
Fires a ball of sound.

d) Doom Bolt
Fires a stream of raw magic.

e) Fire Ball
Fires a ball of flame, doing fire damage.

f) Teleport Other
Teleports the targeted creature to somewhere random on the level.

g) Word of Destruction
Causes the roof to collapse around you, much like the effects of an earthquake.

h) Invoke Chaos
Fires a ball of raw chaos.

G’harne Fragments

a) Polymorph Other
Turns the target into a random creature.

b) Chain Lightning
Fires bolts of lightning in eight directions, doing electricity damage.

c) Arcane Binding
Recharges a wand, staff or rod.

d) Disintegrate
Fires a powerful ball of disintegrating energy that destroys monsters, items, and walls alike.

e) Alter Reality
Completely alters reality, placing you in a different (but similar difficulty) dungeon level.

f) Polymorph Self
Invokes chaos into your own body, warping it in a random way.

g) Chaos Branding
Invokes chaos into your weapon, changing it.

h) Summon Demon
Summons a friendly demon to help you.

The Book of Azathoth

a) Beam of Gravity
Fires a beam of elemental gravity.

b) Meteor Swarm
Makes a number of meteors appear and explode in your vicinity.

c) Flame Strike
Fires an intense ball of flame, doing massive amounts of fire damage.

d) Call Chaos
Fires a random beam or ball of energy.

e) Shard Ball
Fires a ball of sharp shards of metal and stone.

f) Mana Storm
Fires a ball of extreme magical energy.

g) Breathe Chaos
Makes you breathe a huge gout of raw chaos.

h) Call the Void
Invokes an extremely destructive series of explosions around yourself that might be very dangerous in an enclosed area.
