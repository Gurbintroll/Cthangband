Warrior-Mages are the ultimate generalists. They are good fighters (although not as good as a Warrior), and versatile spell casters (although not as good as a Mage). They pay for this versatility by having the largest experience cost to increase in level of any of the classes.

Warrior-Mages can always cast spells from the Folk realm and a second realm of their choice.

They cast these spells as an intelligence based divine spell caster, and as such they choose which spells to learn from their realms when they increase in level.
