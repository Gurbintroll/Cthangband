High mages are intelligence based arcane spell casters who specialise in a single realm of magic. As arcane casters, high mages can choose which spells they receive when they research. Whichever realm they choose, they are the best casters of that realm and they receive a 25% bonus to the normal vril that they would receive for their intelligence and level.

However, they make up for this specialism by being unable to cast spells from other realms and being very weak in combat when not casting spells.
