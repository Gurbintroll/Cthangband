Folk Spells

Spells are presented here ordered by book, reflecting how difficult each spell is to cast. Unlike the other realms of magic, which contain two common books of spells of increasing difficulty which can be bought in town and a further two rare books of assorted spells that can only be found in the dungeons, all four folk books are common and can be bought in town. Similarly, all four books contain spells of increasing difficulty.

The level at which each individual spell is unlocked and the vril cost to cast each spell is not given here, since it will vary with each class that has access to this realm.

Cantrips For Beginners

a) Zap
Fires a bolt of electricity.

b) Wizard Lock
Locks a nearby door.

c) Detect Invisibility
Reveals the location of nearby invisible creatures.

d) Detect Monsters
Reveals the location of nearby creatures.

e) Blink
Teleports you to a random nearby location.

f) Light Area
Permanently lights the nearby area, possibly damaging creatures.

g) Trap & Door Destruction
Destroys any adjacent doors or traps.

h) Cure Light Wounds
Heals 2d8 damage and reduces bleeding.

Minor Magicks

a) Detect Doors & Traps
Detects all nearby secret doors, traps and stairs.

b) Phlogiston
Provides extra fuel for a light source.

c) Detect Treasure
Detects nearby money and seams of treasure.

d) Detect Enchantment
Detects nearby magical items.

e) Detect Objects
Reveals the location of nearby objects.

f) Cure Poison
Removes all poison from your system.

g) Resist Cold
Provides a temporary resistance to cold.

h) Resist Fire
Provides a temporary resistance to fire.

Major Magicks

a) Resist Lightning
Provides a temporary resistance to electricity.

b) Resist Acid
Provides a temporary resistance to acid.

c) Cure Medium Wounds
Cures 4d8 damage and severely reduces bleeding.

d) Teleport
Teleports you to a random location on the level.

e) Stone to Mud
Turns a square of rock into loose mud, effectively removing it.

f) Ray of Light
Creates a beam of permanent light, possibly damaging creatures.

g) Satisfy Hunger
Removes all hunger, leaving you comfortably full.

h) See Invisible
Allows you to see invisible creatures for a short while.

Magicks of Mastery

a) Recharging
Recharges a wand, staff or rod.

b) Teleport Level
Teleports you off the level you are on, onto either the one above or the one below.

c) Identify
Identifies the basic abilities of an item.

d) Teleport Away
Teleports a creature to a random place on the level.

e) Elemental Ball
Fires a ball of a random element (fire, cold, acid or electricity).

f) Detection
Detects secret doors, stairs, traps, treasure, objects and monsters (including invisible ones).

g) Word of Recall
Teleports you back to the town, or - if you are in the town - back to the deepest level you have explored.

h) Clairvoyance
Gives you temporary ESP.
