Druids are divine spell casters who draw their power from nature. They are experts at casting spells from the nature realm, but are unable to cast spells from other realms.

As divine casters, druids are granted random spells when they research (at the library) and use wisdom as a spell casting score. As a type of priest, druids are unable to use edged weapons unless those weapons are blessed.
