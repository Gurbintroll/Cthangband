Monks are melee combatants who attack without weapons. Although they can use weapons, they get special attacks when not using them. These attacks get more powerful as the monk increases in level and the monk also increases in speed as they increase in level.

As well as their physical abilities, monks are able to use magic. They are wisdom based divine spell casters who cast spells from either the corporeal, tarot, or chaos realm. They are not as good at spell casting as other casting classes, although they are better with the corporeal realm that either of the others.
