Martial Arts

When attacking without a weapon, a monk or mysticcharacter gets special martial arts attacks. These start with just a single punch at level 1 and culminating in a Crushing Blow at level 48.

Each time you make a martial arts attack, the game chooses an attack at random from those of your level or below (with equal chance of each), and then rolls a die with the same number of sides as your level. If this die is less than the difficulty of the attack then the attack is re-selected. This may result in the same attack being selected and rolled for again.

For example, let's say that a 23rd level character is making a martial arts attack, and the game randomly selects the 'Double Kick' attack from the list of attacks of 23rd level and lower ('Punch' to 'Cat's Claw'). This has a difficulty of 15, so the game rolls a 23-sided die and if it gets a 14 or lower (i.e. less than the difficulty) on the roll then it re-selects the attack; otherwise it proceeds with the double kick.

The game makes this selection once per seven levels your character has, taking the best of the chosen attacks as the one you will use.

However, if you are stunned or confused you can only do basic 'Punch' attacks.

Note that the 'Ankle Kick' attack may only slow opponents that have legs. Against opponents that do not have legs, this attack will be simply described as a 'kick' and will not do its slowing. However, the attack will still do the full 3d4 damage rather than the 1d6 damage done by a normal kick.
