Tarot Spells

Spells are presented here ordered by book. This does not necessarily reflect how difficult each spell is to cast. Although within the first two books (Conjuring and Tricks and Card Mastery) the spells are ordered in increasing difficulty, each of the two rare books that can only be found in the dungeons (Eltdown Shards and Celeano Fragments) contain both easy and difficult spells.

The level at which each individual spell is unlocked and the vril cost to cast each spell is not given here, since it will vary with each class that has access to this realm.

Conjuring and Tricks

a) Phase Door
Teleports you to a random nearby location.

b) Mind Blast
Fires a bolt of psychic energy.

c) Tarot Draw
Invokes a random (but usually beneficial) effect from a Tarot card.

d) Reset Recall
Resets the depth that you will go to via Word of Recall spells (and scrolls) to the current level.

e) Teleport
Teleports you to a random location on the level.

f) Dimension Door
Teleports you to a nearby location that you specify.

g) Planar Spying
Gives you temporary ESP.

h) Teleport Away
Teleports a creature to a random place on the level.

Card Mastery

a) Summon Object
Teleports a nearby object to your hand.

b) Summon Animal
Summons a friendly animal to help you.

c) Phantasmal Servant
Creates a magical beast to serve you.

d) Summon Monster
Summons a random monster to help you.

e) Conjure Elemental
Summons an elemental to help you.

f) Teleport Level
Teleports you off the level you are on, onto either the one above or the one below.

g) Word of Recall
Teleports you back to the town, or - if you are in the town - down to the deepest dungeon level that you have yet visited.

h) Banish
Teleports all nearby monsters to elsewhere on the level.

Eltdown Shards

a) Joker Card
Summons a bizarre creature to help you.

b) Summon Spiders
Summons spiders to help you.

c) Summon Reptiles
Summons reptiles to help you.

d) Summon Hounds
Summons elemental hounds to help you.

e) Planar Branding
Permanently makes your weapon a ‘Planar Weapon’, attuned to the cosmos.

f) Planar Being
Attunes you with the planes, altering your body.

g) Death Dealing
Banishes all nearby living creatures.

h) Summon Reaver
Summons a Black Reaver to help you.

Celeano Fragments

a) Planar Divination
Detects secret doors, stairs, traps, treasure, objects and monsters (including invisible ones).

b) Planar Lore
Fully identifies an item.

c) Summon Undead
Summons an undead creature to help you.

d) Summon Dragon
Summons a dragon to help you.

e) Mass Summons
Summons multiple monsters to help you.

f) Summon Demon
Summons a demon to help you.

g) Summon Ancient Dragon
Summons an ancient dragon to help you.

h) Summon Greater Undead
Summons a greater undead to help you.
