Charisma

Charisma is a combination of your appearance, personality, and willpower. Charisma is the primary spell casting ability score for mentalism, and the Vril per Level, Power Failure Reduction, and Min Power Failure scores only apply to Mindcrafters and Mystics.

Price Modifier
When buying any item or service in the shops, the price you pay will be be multiplied by this percentage. When selling an item, the price will be divided by this percentage.

Vril per Level
This is the amount of vril per level that you have when fully rested.

Power Failure Reduction
When using a mentalism power, each power has a basic chance of failure. This chance of failure is reduced by 3% per character level and also by the amount shown here for your charisma score.

Min Power Failure
No matter how much the chance of failure when using a mentalism power has been reduced, it can't be reduced below this value.
