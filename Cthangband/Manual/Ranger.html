Rangers are wilderness trackers who specialise in ranged combat. They are also able to use magic, and although versatile magicians their spells are slow to learn and expensive to cast.

Rangers can always cast spells from the Nature realm and a second realm of their choice (except Sorcery).

They cast these spells as a wisdom based divine spell caster, and as such they receive random spells from their realms when they increase in level.
