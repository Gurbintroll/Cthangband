Mystics are melee combatants who attack without weapons. Although they can use weapons, they get special attacks when not using them. These attacks get more powerful as the mystic increases in level and the mystic also increases in speed as they increase in level.

As well as their physical abilities, mystics are able to produce magical effects using only the power of their mind, without the need for learning spells from spell books. Mystics are wisdom based casters, although they do not research spells - instead automatically knowing the various mentalism talents when they are high enough level to use them.
