Chosen ones are those who are selected by the Great Ones to act as champions against evil. Although a chosen one lacks the ability to cast spells, they gain a wide variety of passive magical abilities as they increase in level.

Chosen ones are moderately good at fighting, and their numerous abilities mean that they are free to select the best magical items to wear and wield because they don't need to carefully select magic items to cover weaknesses in their resistances.
