Life Spells

Spells are presented here ordered by book. This does not necessarily reflect how difficult each spell is to cast. Although within the first two books (Book of Common Prayer and High Mass) the spells are ordered in increasing difficulty, each of the two rare books that can only be found in the dungeons (Dhol Chants and Ponape Scripture) contain both easy and difficult spells.

The level at which each individual spell is unlocked and the mana cost to cast each spell is not given here, since it will vary with each class that has access to this realm.

Book of Common Prayer

a) Detect Evil
Detects all evil creatures that are nearby.

b) Cure Light Wounds
Cures you of 2d10 damage, and reduces bleeding.

c) Bless
Blesses you, giving you a +5 bonus to armour class and a +10 bonus to hit.

d) Remove Fear
Stops you from being afraid.

e) Call Light
Lights the area that you are in with a permanent light - possibly damaging some creatures.

f) Detect Traps and Secret Doors
Finds all traps, stairs and secret doors in your surrounding area.

g) Cure Medium Wounds
Cures you of 4d10 damage, and reduces bleeding.

h) Satisfy Hunger
Removes all hunger, leaving you comfortably full.

High Mass

a) Remove Curse
Removes the minor curses from many objects, allowing them to be taken off.

b) Cure Poison
Removes all poison from your system.

c) Cure Critical Wounds
Heals 8d10 damage, and completely stops bleeding or stunning.

d) Sense Unseen
Allows you to see invisible creatures for a short while.

e) Holy Orb
Fires a damaging ball of holy fire.

f) Protection from Evil
Puts up a barrier around yourself that has a chance of stopping attacks from evil creatures.

g) Healing
Heals 300 damage and completely stops bleeding and stunning.

h) Elder Sign
Places a sigil on the floor that many creatures will be unable to pass.

Dhol Chants

a) Exorcism
Banishes demons and undead, and scares other evil creatures.

b) Dispel Curse
Removes more powerful curses from objects.

c) Dispel Undead & Demons
Triple strength banishment against undead and demonic creatures.

d) Day of the Dove
Befriends all nearby monsters.

e) Dispel Evil
Quadruple strength banishment against all evil creatures.

f) Banish
Teleports away all nearby evil creatures to elsewhere on the level.

g) Holy Word
Banishes evil, heals 1000 damage, removes bleeding, removes fear, removes poison and cures stunning.

h) Warding True
Surrounds you with Elder Signs.

Ponape Scripture

a) Heroism
Makes you feel heroic, giving you a +12 bonus to hit and 10 extra hit points. Also removes all fear.

b) Prayer
Like Bless, except that it lasts longer.

c) Bless Weapon
Makes a weapon blessed - and thus usable by priests. This also removes any curse on the weapon. It is dangerous to use this spell on artifacts, as it may damage them.

d) Restoration
Removes any drain or damage from any of your abilities, and removes any experience drain.

e) Healing True
Heals 2000 damage, all bleeding and all stunning.

f) Holy Vision
Fully identifies an item.

g) Divine Intervention
This banishes, stuns, confuses, scares, slows and paralyses monsters that surround you, as well as healing you and hasting you to allow you a retreat.

h) Holy Invulnerability
Makes you completely immune to damage for a short period of time.
