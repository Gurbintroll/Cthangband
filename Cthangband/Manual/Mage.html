Mages are the default choice for those who wish to play a magic based character. They are intelligence based arcane spell casters and can therefore choose which spells they research when they increase in level. Mages can choose any two realms of magic to cast spells from. As characters who primarily rely on magic, mages are weak in melee combat, and as with other arcane casters they are unable to wear heavy armour without it reducing their vis.
