Mindcrafters are able to produce magical effects using only the power of their mind, without the need for learning spells from spell books. Mindcrafters are wisdom based casters, although they do not research spells - instead automatically knowing the various mentalism powers when they are high enough level to use them.

Outside of their magical abilities, mindcrafters are somewhat average at fighting, being better than dedicated mages but not as good as pure warriors.
