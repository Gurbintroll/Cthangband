Sorcery Spells

Spells are presented here ordered by book. This does not necessarily reflect how difficult each spell is to cast. Although within the first two books (Beginner's Handbook and Master Sorcerer's Handbook) the spells are ordered in increasing difficulty, each of the two rare books that can only be found in the dungeons (Unaussprechlichen Kulten and Liber Ivonis) contain both easy and difficult spells.

The level at which each individual spell is unlocked and the mana cost to cast each spell is not given here, since it will vary with each class that has access to this realm.

Beginner’s Handbook

a) Detect Monsters
Detects the presence of all monsters in the nearby area.

b) Phase Door
Teleports you to a random location within 100’

c) Detect Doors and Traps
Reveals any doors, stairs and traps that are in the nearby area.

d) Light Area
Creates a permanent light in your surrounding area, possibly damaging creatures.

e) Confuse Monster
Confuses a nearby (non-undead) monster.

f) Teleport
Teleports you to a random location on the level.

g) Sleep Monster
Sends a nearby monster to sleep.

h) Recharging
Recharges a wand, rod or staff.

Master Sorcerer’s Handbook

a) Magic Mapping
Reveals the layout of your nearby surroundings.

b) Identify
Identifies the basic abilities of an item.

c) Slow Monster
Slows down the movement and attacks of a nearby monster.

d) Mass Sleep
Sends all nearby monsters to sleep.

e) Teleport Away
Teleports a nearby monster away from you, to a random place on the level.

f) Haste Self
Speeds your movement for a limited duration.

g) Detection True
Detects secret doors, stairs, traps, treasure, objects and monsters (including invisible ones).

h) Identify True
Fully identifies an item.

Unaussprechlichen Kulten

a) Detect Objects and Treasure
Reveals the location of all objects and money.

b) Detect Enchantment
Reveals the location of magical items.

c) Charm Monster
Makes a nearby monster friendly.

d) Dimension Door
Teleports you to a location of your choice on the current level.

e) Sense Minds
Gives you ESP for a short duration.

f) Self Knowledge
Reveals knowledge about yourself and your abilities (including those given by items).

g) Teleport Level
Teleports you completely off the level - either to the level below or the level above.

h) Word of Recall
Teleports you back to the town, or - if you are in the town - back to the deepest level you have explored.

Liber Ivonis

a) Stasis
Freezes a monster in time, preventing it from taking any actions for a while.

b) Telekinesis
Picks up an object from a distance.

c) Yellow Sign
Leaves a sigil on the floor that will explode when a creature walks over it.

d) Clairvoyance
Reveals the layout of the entire level, and gives you ESP for a short duration.

e) Enchant Weapon
Increases the magical bonuses on your weapon.

f) Enchant Armour
Increases the magical bonuses on a piece of your armour.

g) Alchemy
Turns an item to gold, giving you approximately a third of the amount you would be able to get by selling it.

h) Globe of Invulnerability
Makes you completely immune to damage for a short while.
