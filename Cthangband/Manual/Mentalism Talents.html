Mentalism Talents

The talents of mentalism work in a simlar way to spells, but they are not found in books and learned. Instead, a mindcrafter or mystic can automatically use any talent for which they have gained the minimum level.

Unlike spells, which usually have a consistent effect with only things like damage increasing based on character level, mentalism talents will often have an increased or changed effect at higher level.

a) Precognition
Initially, this detects monsters. It then starts also detecting traps (min lev = 5), invisible creatures (min lev = 15), revealing the layout of your surrounding area (min lev = 20), providing you with temporary ESP (min lev = 25) and revealing the layout of the whole level (min lev = 45).

b) Neural Blast
This fires a bolt of psychic energy. Sometimes, this bolt will explode into a ball. It is more likely to become a ball if you are higher level.

c) Minor Displacement
This will teleport you to a random nearby location. Once you are practiced with it (min lev = 25), you can choose your destination.

d) Major Displacement
This will teleport you to a random place on the level. With practice (min lev = 30), it will also scatter any nearby monsters once you arrive, teleporting them away from your new location and leaving you in safety.

e) Domination
This will make all nearby monsters your friends. With practice (min lev = 30), you can use this power at a distance.

f) Pulverise
This will fire a ball of sonic energy.

g) Character Armour
This will provide a temporary boost to your armour class. With practice it will also give you temporary resistance to acid (min lev = 14), fire (min lev = 20), cold (min lev = 25), electricity (min lev = 30) and poison (min lev = 35).

h) Psychometry
This gives you a vague idea of the usefulness of an object. With practice (min lev = 40), you can identify the magical function of the object.

i) Mind Wave
This creates a ball of psychic energy around yourself. With practice (min lev = 25), this ball can be fired at a distance.

j) Adrenaline Channeling
This stops you from being afraid or stunned, heals you slightly, gives you a temporary boost to your speed, and boosts your fighting ability. With practice (min lev = 35), the fighting bonus is increased.

k) Psychic Drain
This fires a ball of negative psychic energy that sucks out the minds of its targets and channels the mental energy back to you, which you absorb as spell points.

l) Telekinetic Wave
This fires a fierce blast of stunning telekinetic energy out in all directions.
