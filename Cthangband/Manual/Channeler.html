Channelers are similar to spell casters. Although they are unable to cast spells themselves, they are able to channel their mana into items. This allows them to use items and expend mana instead of expending charges or usages of the item they are using. For example, a channeler could use a Wand of Magic Missiles to shoot magic missiles and - providing they have mana - the wand would never run out of charges.

Channelers are not good fighters, relying on their item use in combat rather than physical attacks.

Channelers derive their mana from their charisma.
