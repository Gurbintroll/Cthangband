Nature Spells

Spells are presented here ordered by book. This does not necessarily reflect how difficult each spell is to cast. Although within the first two books (Call of the Wild and Nature Mastery) the spells are ordered in increasing difficulty, each of the two rare books that can only be found in the dungeons (Revelations of Glaaki and Cthaat Aquadingen) contain both easy and difficult spells.

The level at which each individual spell is unlocked and the mana cost to cast each spell is not given here, since it will vary with each class that has access to this realm.

Call of the wild

a) Detect Creatures
Reveals the presence of all nearby creatures.

b) First Aid
Cures you of 2d8 damage, and reduces your bleeding.

c) Detect Doors and Traps
Reveals the location of any nearby secret doors, traps or stairs.

d) Foraging
Produces enough food to fill you up.

e) Daylight
Lights your immediate area with a permanent light, possibly damaging creatures.

f) Animal Taming
Makes an animal your friend.

g) Resist Environment
Gives you a temporary resistance to cold, fire and electricity.

h) Cure Wounds & Poison
Removes all poison and bleeding.

Nature Mastery

a) Stone to Mud
Turns a square of rock into loose mud, effectively removing it.

b) Lightning Bolt
Fires a bolt of lightning doing electricity damage.

c) Nature Awareness
Reveals the layout of the local area, as well as finding secret doors, stairs and traps. Also reveals the location of any nearby monsters.

d) Frost Bolt
Fires a bolt frost doing cold damage.

e) Ray of Sunlight
Creates a beam of permanent light, possibly damaging creatures.

f) Entangle
Causes vines to grow from the floor, slowing all nearby monsters.

g) Summon Animal
Summons an animal ally to help you.

h) Herbal Healing
Cures 1000 damage, and removes bleeding, poison and stunning.

Revelations of Glaaki

a) Door Building
Makes a door to protect you from anything which might be following you.

b) Stair Building
Creates a staircase at your feet for quick escape.

c) Stone Skin
Adds 50 to your armour class for a short while.

d) Resistance True
Gives you a temporary resistance to acid, cold, fire, electricity and poison.

e) Animal Friendship
Makes all nearby animals your friends.

f) Stone Tell
Fully identifies an item.

g) Wall of Stone
Creates a wall of stone to protect you from anything which might be following you.

h) Protect from Corrosion
Permanently protects an item from acid damage.

Cthaat Aquadingen

a) Earthquake
Creates a large earthquake centered on your location, strong enough to collapse the roof. You are protected from the effects of this spell, safe in the epicenter.

b) Whirlwind Attack
Allows you to attack all adjacent creatures in melee.

c) Blizzard
Creates a ball of frost, dealing cold damage.

d) Lightning Storm
Creates a ball of lightning, doing electricity damage.

e) Whirlpool
Creates a whirling ball of water, doing elemental water damage.

f) Call Sunlight
Creates a ball of elemental light, and also lights the whole level permanently.

g) Elemental Branding
Permanently engulfs the striking surface of a weapon in an element, causing it to do more damage.

h) Nature’s Wrath
Severely damages all nearby creatures and causes an earthquake.
