Death Spells

Spells are presented here ordered by book. This does not necessarily reflect how difficult each spell is to cast. Although within the first two books (Black Prayers and Black Mass) the spells are ordered in increasing difficulty, each of the two rare books that can only be found in the dungeons (Cultes Des Goules and The Necronomicon) contain both easy and difficult spells.

The level at which each individual spell is unlocked and the vis cost to cast each spell is not given here, since it will vary with each class that has access to this realm.

Black Prayers

a) Detect Unlife
Reveals the location of any nearby undead or demonic creatures.

b) Malediction
Fires a bolt of hell fire that may also scare, confuse or stun creatures.

c) Detect Evil
Reveals the location of any nearby evil creatures.

d) Stinking Cloud
Fires a ball of poison gas.

e) Black Sleep
Sends a nearby monster to sleep.

f) Resist Poison
Gives you a temporary resistance to poison.

g) Horrify
Scares and stuns a nearby monster.

h) Enslave Undead
Makes an undead your friend.

Black Mass

a) Orb of Entropy
Fires a life draining ball of energy.

b) Nether Bolt
Fires a bolt of nether.

c) Terror
Scares away all nearby monsters.

d) Vampiric Drain
Drains the life from a nearby creature, both healing you and sating your hunger.

e) Poison Branding
Permanently coats your weapon with poison.

f) Dispel Good
Banishes good creatures.

g) Carnage
Destroys all creatures of a chosen type on a level. Unique creatures are teleported off the level, rather than destroyed. You get no experience for monsters killed with Carnage.

h) Restore Life
Restores any drained experience you have.

Cultes Des Goules

a) Berserk
Sends you berserk giving you 30 extra hit points, +24 to hit and -10 to armour class.

b) Invoke Spirits
Has a random (but usually beneficial) effect. The higher level you are, the more likely it is to be useful.

c) Dark Bolt
Fires a bolt of darkness.

d) Battle Frenzy
Sends you berserk and also hastes you for a short while.

e) Vampirism True
Drains a large amount of life from a target and heals you by the amount drained.

f) Vampiric Branding
Permanently turns your weapon into a life-draining vampiric blade.

g) Darkness Storm
Fires a ball of darkness.

h) Mass Carnage
Destroys all non-unique creatures on the level. You get no experience for monsters killed with Mass Carnage.

The Necronomicon

a) Death Ray
Fires a ray that will kill almost any living creature.

b) Raise the Dead
Creates undead servants to help you.

c) Esoteria
Identifies (with varying accuracy) an item you are carrying.

d) Word of Death
Kills or damages all nearby living creatures.

e) Evocation
Banishes, teleports away, and scares all nearby living creatures.

f) Hellfire
Fires a huge ball of hell fire.

g) Annihilation
Destroys all non-unique creatures on the level, absorbing their essence as spell points. You get no experience for monsters killed with Annihilation.

h) Wraithform
Temporarily makes you intangible so that you can walk through walls.
