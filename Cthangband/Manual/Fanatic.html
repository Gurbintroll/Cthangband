Fanatics are militant followers of a Great Old One, who are granted rewards (and occasional punishments!) by their patron each time they increase in level.

Although primarily melee combatants, fanatics are also granted spells from the Chaos realm. They cast these spells as a wisdom based divine spell casters, and as such they receive random spells from their realm when they increase in level.
