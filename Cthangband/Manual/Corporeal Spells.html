Corporeal Spells

Spells are presented here ordered by book. This does not necessarily reflect how difficult each spell is to cast. Although within the first two books (Basic Chi Flow and Yogic Mastery) the spells are ordered in increasing difficulty, each of the two rare books that can only be found in the dungeons (De Vermis Mysteriis and Pnakotic Manuscripts) contain both easy and difficult spells.

The level at which each individual spell is unlocked and the vril cost to cast each spell is not given here, since it will vary with each class that has access to this realm.

Basic Chi Flow

a) Cure Light Wounds
Heals 2d10 damage and reduces bleeding.

b) Blink
Teleports you to a random nearby location.

c) Bravery
Stops you being afraid.

d) Bat’s Sense
Reveals the layout of your nearby surroundings.

e) Eagle’s Vision
Detects secret doors, stairs, traps, treasure, objects and monsters (including invisible ones).

f) Mind Vision
Gives you temporary ESP.

g) Cure Medium Wounds
Cures 4d10 damage and severely reduces bleeding.

h) Satisfy Hunger
Removes all hunger, leaving you comfortably full.

Yogic Mastery

a) Burn Resistance
Gives you temporary resistance to fire, acid and electricity.

b) Detoxify
Removes all poison from your system.

c) Cure Critical Wounds
Heals 8d10 damage, and completely stops bleeding or stunning.

d) See Invisible
Allows you to see invisible creatures for a short while.

e) Teleport
Teleports you to a random location on the level.

f) Haste
Temporarily speeds up your movement.

g) Healing
Heals 300 damage and completely stops bleeding and stunning.

h) Resist True
Gives you a temporary resistance to acid, cold, fire, electricity and poison.

De Vermis Mysteriis

a) Horrific Visage
Scares and stuns a nearby monster.

b) See Magic
Detects nearby magical items.

c) Stone Skin
Adds 50 to your armour class for a short while.

d) Move Body
Teleports you to a nearby location that you specify.

e) Corrupt Body
Warps your body in a random manner, giving you a mutation.

f) Know Self
Reveals knowledge about yourself and your abilities (including those given by items).

g) Teleport Level
Teleports you off the level you are on, onto either the one above or the one below.

h) Word of Recall
Teleports you back to the town, or - if you are in the town - back to the deepest level you have explored.

Pnakotic Manuscripts

a) Heroism
Makes you feel heroic, giving you a +12 bonus to hit and 10 extra hit points. Also removes all fear.

b) Wraithform
Temporarily makes you intangible so that you can walk through walls.

c) Attune to Magic
Fully identifies an item.

d) Restore Body
Heals damage to all six ability scores.

e) Healing True
Heals 2000 damage, all bleeding and all stunning.

f) Hypnotic Eyes
Makes a monster your friend.

g) Restore Soul
Restores any drained experience.

h) Invulnerability
Makes you completely immune to damage for a short while.
